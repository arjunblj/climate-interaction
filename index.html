An interactive exploration of data. Charted above is C02 emission data over time (source) as well as cost of OPEC oil. Most interestingly, the visualization can be interacted with, through comments.

One of my aims was to create a prototype of a public art piece that people can interact with. I believe one of the reasons why public displays (e.g. graffiti, murals) are so effective is because people interact with the finished product, instead of merely "looking" and consuming.

My hope is that you (the consumer) comment but my greater desire is to create a dialogue around how this piece can be extended to be made more effective. All of code and data sets used are open-source (on GitHub above).

To use: double-click above to comment. Use the scrollbar to zoom in on a year, or click-and-drag to explore different portions of axes.
